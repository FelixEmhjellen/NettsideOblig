A project for the future of Oslo, by the people of Oslo

Our vision is to connect the people of Gjersrud-Stensrud to the rest Oslo fast, safe and sustainable. Monorails is being used in several major cities like Kuala Lumpur, Las Vegas, Sao Paolo etc with positive effects. We hope to be able to remove traffic from the streets, thereby also removing noise and air pollution. We also aim to be able to give the residents of Gjersrud Stensrud a enjoyable and comfortable commute to Oslo. From the monorails large windows, they can enjoy the view of the fjord as they head to the city.
